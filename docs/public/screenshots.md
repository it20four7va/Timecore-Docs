This page allows the user to view their tasks, work hours, and screenshots for a specified time. It could be filtered daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly.

**NOTE:** Server automatically deletes screenshots that are more than three (3) weeks old.

## **How to view screenshots?**
   
1. On the **‘View Screenshots’** page, enter the desired **‘From Date’** and **‘To Date’** and click the **‘Refresh’** button. 

2. Once the page reloads, choose the entry that you wish to view the captured screenshots on the table.

3. Click on the corresponding **‘Actions (...)’** button and select the **‘View Screenshots’** option.

4. The **‘Screenshots’** page is displayed. You may click on the **‘Back to Session List’** button to view other recorded screenshots.

## **How to delete screenshots?**

1. On the **‘View Screenshots’** page, enter the desired **‘From Date’** and **‘To Date’** and click the **‘Refresh’** button.

2. Once the page reloads, choose the entry that you wish to delete a/the captured screenshot/s on the table.

3. Click on the corresponding **‘Actions (...)’** button and select the **‘View Screenshots’** option.

4. On the **‘Screenshots’** page, click the corresponding **‘Delete’** icon of the screenshot/s that you wish to delete.

A pop-up message is displayed. Click the **‘OK’** button to continue deleting or the **‘Cancel’** button to cancel the process. Upon clicking the **‘OK’** button, a successful deletion confirmation message should be displayed on the screen.

## **How to delete a session?**

1. On the **‘View Screenshots’** page, enter the desired ‘From Date’ and ‘To Date’ and click the ‘Refresh’ button.

2. Once the page reloads, choose the session that you wish to delete on the table.

3. Click on the corresponding **‘Actions (...)’** button and select the **‘Delete Session’** option.

A pop-up message is displayed. Click the **‘OK’** button to continue deleting or the **‘Cancel’** button to cancel the process (Figure 14). Upon clicking the **‘OK’** button, a successful deletion confirmation message should be displayed on the screen.
