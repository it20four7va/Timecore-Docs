## How to read the site Dashboard?

- **Autolog Summary**

    This is the chart representation of the Total Hours of Work of the day

- **Manual Log Summary**
  
    This is the chart representation of the Manual Hours Work of the week.

- **Leave Summary**
  
    This is the chart representation of the Available Paid Time-Offs for one whole year.

- **Total Hours Today**
  
    This shows the total work hours recorded on the 20four7VA Time Tracker Desktop App of the day.

- **Manual Hours This Week**
  
    This shows the total approved manual hours of the week.

- **Available PTOs**
  
    This shows the total available paid time-offs for one whole year.

- **Weekly Timesheet**
  
    This shows the total weekly timesheet record of the user.

- **My Clients**
  
    This shows the client(s) details under the user.
