The 20four7VA TimeTracker is a proprietary software made in order to manage the user's work hours in a more robust and smooth manner. It will help managers and clients track and monitor the VA's work on a regular basis.

**Software OS Versions -** The  TimeTracker comes in 3 operating system versions as of now,  Windows (7,8,10), Mac and Linux.

**Download -** In order to Download the **Windows / Mac / Linux version**, users can type in: [https://timetracker.20four7va.co](https://timetracker.20four7va.co) in the address bar and go to the **Menu Downloads > Windows/ Mac/Linux** as per their computer systems.

## INSTALLATION PROCEDURE

1. Once you click on the Downloads > Windows section from the top menu bar on site it will take you to the following page:

2. On the **‘Download’** Windows File page. Click on the **‘Download’** button and the software will download in the bottom left corner of the screen as shown below:

   Note: We are taking Chrome Browser as a Standard. The download location can be different if you use any other browsers like Firefox, Safari, Opera, or IE.

3. Once the download has been completed, click it to open the downloaded executable file. This pop-up message might be displayed. Click on the **‘More info’** link and click on the **‘Run anyway’** button.

4. Click on the **‘Install’** and the software will install automatically. The login screen will automatically show up, as well. Enter the username & password that was sent to your webmail account and click on **‘Login’**.

5. The app will load relevant projects and tasks and you will see the following screen post-loading.

6. Before you start the timer you must Select a **‘Project’** and its associated **‘Task’**. Then finally click on **‘Start Timer’** .

7. You are done. Congrats! TimeTracker will start recording & tracking your system!

8. Upon completion of your daily work / while taking a break you can click on **‘Stop Timer’** & click on **‘Log Out’**.
