The ‘Reports’ page is where the users could access their End of Day (EOD) Report, Submit Speed Test, TimeSheets, and other reports.

1. **How to send your EOD report?**
   
   - On the menu, click the **‘Reports’** and then the **‘Send My EOD’** sub-menu.
   - The **‘Send your EOD’** page is displayed.
   - Pick a date and click on the **‘Refresh’** button
   - Fill out all the other fields (required or optional) on the **‘Send your EOD’** page and click the Submit button.
   - Upon submitting the form on the **‘Send your EOD’** page, a successful confirmation message is displayed. To submit another EOD Report, you may pick another date again, hit the **‘Refresh’** button, and fill out the form.
