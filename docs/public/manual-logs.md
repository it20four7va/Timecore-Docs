This page deals with the different logs of the user such as the manual logs, overtime logs, and leaves. You could file the said logs on this page and monitor for its result if it’s approved or not.

1. **How to add a manual log?**
   
      - On the menu, click the **‘Manual Logs’** and then the ‘Add Manual Log’ sub-menu.
      - The **‘Manual Log’** page is displayed. 
      - Fill out all the required fields on the **‘Manual Log’** page and click the **‘Save And Upload’** button.
      - Upon providing all the required fields and clicking the **‘Save And Upload’** button on the **‘Manual Log’** page, a successful confirmation message is displayed. 

2. **How to view added manual logs?**

      - On the menu, click the **‘Manual Log’** and then the **‘Manage Manual Logs’** sub-menu.
      - The **‘Manage Manual Logs’** page is displayed. You may edit the **‘From’** and **‘To’** date pickers to filter the manual logs search result and check for its **‘Status’**. 

3. **How to add overtime hours?**
   
      - On the menu, click the **‘Manual Logs’** and then the **‘Add Overtime Hours’** sub-menu.
      - The **‘Over Time Log’** page is displayed. 
      - Fill out all the required fields on the **‘Over Time Log’** page and click the **‘Save And Upload’** button.
      - Upon providing all the required fields and clicking the **‘Save And Upload’** button on the **‘Over Time Log’** page, a successful confirmation message is displayed. 

4. **How to view added overtime logs?**

     - On the menu, click the **‘Manual Log’** and then the **‘Manage Overtime Logs’** sub-menu.
     - The **‘Manage Overtime Logs’** page is displayed. You may edit the **‘From’** and **‘To’** date pickers to filter the overtime logs search result and check for its **‘Status’**. 

5. **How to apply for a leave?**

      - On the menu, click the **‘Manual Logs’** and then the **‘Apply for Leave’** sub-menu.
      - The **'Apply for Leave’** page is displayed. 
      - Fill out all the required fields on the **‘Apply for Leave’** page and click the **‘Submit Leave Application’** button.
      - Upon providing all the required fields and clicking the **‘Submit Leave Application’** on the **‘Apply for Leave’** page, a successful confirmation message is displayed. 
 
6. **How to view applied leaves?**
   
      - On the menu, click the **‘Manual Log’** and then the **‘Manage Leaves’** sub-menu.
      - The **‘Manage Leaves’** page is displayed. You may edit the **‘From’** and **‘To’** date pickers to filter the leaves logs search result and check for its **‘Status’**. 
     
    The **‘Reports’** page is where the users could access their End of Day (EOD) Report, Submit Speed Test, TimeSheets, and other reports.

7. **How to send your EOD report?**

     - On the menu, click the **‘Reports’** and then the **‘Send My EOD’** sub-menu.
     - The **‘Send your EOD’** page is displayed. 
     - Pick a date and click on the **‘Refresh’** button.
     - Fill out all the other fields (required or optional) on the **‘Send your EOD’** page and click the Submit button.
     - Upon submitting the form on the **‘Send your EOD’** page, a successful confirmation message is displayed. To submit another EOD Report, you may pick another date again, hit the **‘Refresh’** button, and fill out the form.

8. **How to submit your speed test?**

    - On the menu, click the **‘Reports’** and then the **‘Submit Speed Test’** sub-menu.
    - The **‘Speedtest’** page is displayed. 
    - Run the speed test by clicking the ‘GO’ button, copy the link and paste it on the **‘Speed Test Result Link:’** input field.
    - Fill out the **‘Internet Service Provider Details’** form and click the **‘Submit Details’** button. Make sure to enter both the **‘Main’** and **‘Backup Internet Connection’** types.
    - Upon submitting the form on the **‘Speedtest’** page, a successful confirmation message is displayed.
    
    **NOTE:** Every first day of the month, the system will ask for the main and backup internet speed test details of the user before fully utilizing the system menus.
